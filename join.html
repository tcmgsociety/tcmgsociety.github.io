Membership

Thank you for considering to join the Technology Management Society! Here are the steps to join.

1. Join Our Discord
2. Next, please change your nickname in this server to your preferred name, this will help us put names to faces when we start to meet each other in workshops.
3. Fill out the google form below. Please make sure you sign into the google form with your TAMU email or you won’t have permission!
4. Please use the verify text channel to let the officers know you completed the form, unfortunately it is a manual process so please give us some time to verify your information and give you access to the rest of the server.
5. Enjoy every perk and opportunity available through our organization!

Dues

Membership requires $25 to be paid by the end of the first month of the semester (fall or spring).
Members can pay $25 a semester or $45 for the full year.
